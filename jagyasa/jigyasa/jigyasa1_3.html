FEATURED CASE STUDY

HOW AERIES HELPED

DEAL SENSING PLATFORM FROM CLEARLAKE

CHALLENGE

For private equity investors, finding the perfect company to invest in is like finding the ideal partner for growth and success. It's crucial to scout, sense, and identify the right firm from a sea of options, but it's a challenging task. Multiple criteria must be used to make the right decision, while also competing with other investors in the process.

SOLUTION

ARI proposed a Deal Sensing Platform, that will help the client in multiple ways to overcome the competition with speed and quality.

  1. Scan the Market - based on criterions to find the shortlist firms
  2. Enable Human Like Inference - decide on behalf of a human which firms to share with the deal team
  3. Enhanced NLP Pipeline - Use NLP as a key aspect of data pipeline, to streamline and collate reports for end users
